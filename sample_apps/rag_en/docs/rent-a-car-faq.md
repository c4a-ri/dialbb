# Blue Ocean Rent-A-Car FAQ

## Reservations

Q. How can I make a reservation?
A. You can make a reservation through our official website, by phone, or at a rental office counter.

Q. Can I make a same-day reservation?
A. Yes, you can reserve on the same day if a vehicle is available.

Q. How far in advance can I make a reservation?
A. Reservations can be made up to 6 months in advance.

Q. Can I rent a car without a reservation if I visit a branch?
A. Yes, if vehicles are available, but we recommend booking in advance.

Q. Can I change my reservation details?
A. Yes, you can change the vehicle type, date and time, branch, and other details before the rental starts.

Q. Is there a fee for changing a reservation?
A. Changes made up to 24 hours before the rental start time are free of charge.

Q. Can I cancel my reservation?
A. Yes, you can cancel through the website or by contacting the branch.

Q. Is there a cancellation fee?
A. A cancellation fee applies if you cancel within 24 hours of the rental start time.

Q. Can I reserve multiple vehicles at once?
A. Yes, multiple vehicles can be reserved for corporate or group use.

Q. I did not receive my reservation confirmation email. What should I do?
A. Please check your spam folder first. If it is not there, contact the branch.

## Eligibility

Q. What do I need to rent a car?
A. You need a valid driver's license, identification, and a payment method.

Q. Can I rent a car if I just got my driver's license?
A. Yes, but some vehicle types require that you have held your license for at least one year.

Q. Can customers under 20 use the service?
A. Yes, customers aged 18 or older with a valid driver's license can use the service.

Q. Can senior customers rent a car?
A. Yes, as long as they have a valid driver's license.

Q. Can I rent with an international driver's permit?
A. Yes, as long as it is a valid international driving permit recognized under the applicable treaty.

Q. Can I rent using only a foreign driver's license?
A. Licenses from some countries and regions can be used together with a Japanese translation.

Q. Can someone else pick up the car for me?
A. In principle, the contract holder or a registered driver must come to the branch in person.

Q. Can multiple people drive the car?
A. Yes, please register them as additional drivers before departure.

Q. Do additional drivers also need a driver's license?
A. Yes, all drivers must have a valid driver's license.

Q. Can students rent a car?
A. Yes, as long as they have a driver's license and identification.

## Vehicle Types

Q. What kinds of vehicles are available?
A. We offer kei cars, compact cars, sedans, minivans, SUVs, commercial vans, and more.

Q. Can I choose a specific vehicle model?
A. Reservations are generally made by vehicle class, but a vehicle model selection option may be available depending on inventory.

Q. Do you have non-smoking cars?
A. All of our vehicles are non-smoking.

Q. Do you have any cars in which smoking is allowed?
A. No, we do not offer vehicles in which smoking is permitted.

Q. Can I rent a hybrid vehicle?
A. Yes, hybrid vehicles are available at some branches.

Q. Do you have electric vehicles?
A. Yes, we offer EVs.

Q. Do you have cars with snow tires?
A. Yes, some vehicles are equipped with snow tires during the winter season only.

Q. Can I rent a child seat?
A. Yes, child seats are available as a paid option.

Q. Do the cars come with a navigation system?
A. Yes, all vehicles are equipped with a navigation system as standard.

Q. Are ETC onboard units installed?
A. Yes, all vehicles are equipped with ETC onboard units. Please bring your own ETC card.

## Rates

Q. How are rental rates determined?
A. Rates are based on the vehicle class, rental duration, options, and coverage plan.

Q. Is fuel included in the rental fee?
A. No, fuel is not included. In principle, the vehicle should be returned with a full tank.

Q. Can I rent a car for only 6 hours?
A. Yes, we offer a 6-hour plan.

Q. Can I rent by the day?
A. Yes, we offer a 24-hour plan.

Q. Are there discounts for long-term rentals?
A. Yes, a long-term discount is available for rentals of 7 days or more.

Q. Do you have corporate rates?
A. Yes, we offer special rates for corporate contracts.

Q. Do you offer student discounts?
A. Yes, some plans are discounted with presentation of a student ID.

Q. Is there a late-night surcharge?
A. A late-night surcharge may apply to departures or returns between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM.

Q. How much is the extension fee?
A. An hourly extension fee applies according to the vehicle class.

Q. Can I request only a quotation?
A. Yes, you can get a free quote on the website.

## Payment

Q. What payment methods do you accept?
A. We accept credit cards, e-money, QR code payments, and cash.

Q. Is a credit card required?
A. In principle, we ask customers to pay by credit card.

Q. Can I pay in cash?
A. Yes, but you will need to present additional identification.

Q. Can I use a debit card?
A. Yes, debit cards of some brands are accepted.

Q. Can you issue a receipt?
A. Yes, receipts can be issued at the branch or through the web statement.

Q. Can I pay by invoice?
A. Invoice billing is available only for customers with a corporate contract.

Q. Do I need to pay at the time of reservation?
A. For web reservations, you can choose advance payment or branch payment.

Q. Can additional charges be incurred after I return the car?
A. Yes, additional charges may apply for extensions, insufficient fuel, vehicle damage, traffic fines, and similar cases.

Q. Can I earn reward points?
A. Yes, if you register as a member, you can earn points based on your spending.

Q. Can I use coupons?
A. Yes, please enter your coupon code when making the reservation.

## Pickup and Return

Q. What procedures are required at pickup?
A. We will check your driver's license, confirm the contract details, and inspect the vehicle condition.

Q. How long does the pickup procedure take?
A. It usually takes about 10 to 15 minutes.

Q. What is required at return?
A. We will inspect the vehicle, check the mileage and fuel level, and settle the charges.

Q. What should I do if I expect to be late returning the car?
A. Please be sure to contact the return branch in advance.

Q. Can I return the car outside business hours?
A. Yes, you can use the after-hours return box.

Q. Can I return the car to a different branch from where I picked it up?
A. Yes, that is possible.

Q. Is there a one-way rental fee?
A. Yes, a one-way fee may apply depending on the distance and branch locations.

Q. Do I need to wash the car before returning it?
A. No, that is not necessary for normal use.

Q. What should I do if I leave something in the car?
A. Please contact the branch where you rented the car. We will guide you once the item is confirmed.

Q. What happens if the branch is crowded when I return the car?
A. We will confirm your return based on your arrival time, so please speak to a staff member.

## Fuel and Charging

Q. Is the car rented out with a full tank?
A. In principle, vehicles are provided with a full tank.

Q. Do I need to return the car with a full tank as well?
A. Yes, please return it with a full tank.

Q. What happens if I cannot refill the tank completely?
A. You will be charged a fuel settlement fee based on our company policy.

Q. How can I confirm what type of fuel the car uses?
A. It is indicated in the vehicle document holder or near the fuel cap.

Q. Can I put premium gasoline in a regular-gasoline car?
A. Please use the fuel type specified for the vehicle.

Q. Do you have diesel vehicles?
A. Yes, some commercial vehicles and SUVs are diesel models.

Q. Are EVs rented out fully charged?
A. In principle, EVs are rented out with a charge level of 80% or higher.

Q. Do EVs need to be fully charged when returned?
A. The required battery level at return depends on the plan.

Q. Can I rent a charging card?
A. Some branches lend EV charging cards.

Q. Do I need to keep the fuel receipt?
A. Yes, please keep it, as we may need to confirm it when you return the car.

## Insurance and Coverage

Q. Is insurance included in the base rate?
A. Yes, the base coverage includes bodily injury, property damage, vehicle coverage, and more.

Q. What is the liability waiver option?
A. It is a plan that waives the deductible amount the customer would otherwise pay in the event of an accident.

Q. Should I enroll in the liability waiver option?
A. We recommend joining for peace of mind in case of an emergency.

Q. What is a non-operation charge?
A. It is compensation for business loss when a vehicle cannot be used for rental service due to an accident or breakdown.

Q. Is there a plan that waives the NOC?
A. Yes, it is waived if you join the Full Support Plan.

Q. Are there cases where insurance does not apply?
A. Yes, unauthorized driving, driving under the influence, failure to report an accident, and gross negligence are excluded.

Q. Is a flat tire covered?
A. Coverage depends on the plan you choose.

Q. Is windshield damage from flying stones covered?
A. It may be covered depending on the conditions of the base or additional coverage.

Q. Am I covered even in a single-vehicle accident?
A. Yes, if you contact both the police and the branch and meet the coverage conditions.

Q. Do I need an accident certificate?
A. Yes, a police report and accident certificate are required for coverage to apply.

## Accidents and Troubles

Q. What should I do if I have an accident?
A. Please provide aid to any injured persons, contact the police, and notify the branch or support center.

Q. Can I settle the matter privately at the scene of an accident?
A. No, you must contact both the police and our company.

Q. What should I do if the car breaks down?
A. Stop in a safe place and contact the support center.

Q. Can you help if the battery dies?
A. Yes, we can arrange roadside assistance.

Q. What should I do if I lock the key inside the car?
A. Please contact the support center. We will arrange unlocking service.

Q. What happens if I lose the key?
A. You may be responsible for the cost of key replacement and vehicle transport.

Q. What should I do if I get a parking ticket?
A. Promptly pay the fine at the police station and present the payment slip when returning the car.

Q. Will the company pay traffic fines for me?
A. No, traffic fines are the customer's responsibility.

Q. What should I do if the vehicle needs to be towed?
A. Contact the support center and follow their instructions.

Q. A warning light came on while driving. What should I do?
A. Stop in a safe place, check the vehicle condition, and then contact the branch.

## Other Services

Q. Can I bring a pet in the car?
A. Yes, but only if you use a dedicated carrier and notify us in advance.

Q. Can I eat or drink inside the car?
A. Light eating and drinking are allowed, but a cleaning fee may be charged if stains or odors remain.

Q. Do you have vehicles suitable for moving?
A. Yes, we offer kei vans, wagons, and commercial vans.

Q. Do you have vehicles that can carry camping gear?
A. Yes, we recommend minivans and SUVs.

Q. Can I sign a corporate contract?
A. Yes, we support end-of-month billing and management of multiple vehicles.

Q. Do you offer monthly rentals?
A. Yes, we offer long-term rental plans on a monthly basis.

Q. How is a rental car different from car sharing?
A. Rental cars are better suited for long hours or long-distance travel, and include in-person support and coverage explanations.

Q. What can I do by registering as a member?
A. You can manage your reservation history, earn points, and use member-only coupons.

Q. Where can I contact you?
A. Please contact any branch or the Blue Ocean Rent-A-Car customer support center.

## Branches

Q. Where are your branches located?
A. They are located at Naha Airport, Kokusai Street, and Kencho-mae.
